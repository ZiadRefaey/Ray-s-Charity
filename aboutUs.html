About Ray's Charity

Our Mission

At Ray’s Charity, our mission is to provide life-changing support to orphans and vulnerable children around the world. We believe that every child deserves love, care, and the opportunity to thrive, regardless of their circumstances. Our values of compassion, empowerment, and community drive everything we do, from our day-to-day work to our long-term goals.

Our History

Our charity was founded in 2008 by a group of passionate volunteers who wanted to make a difference in the lives of orphans and vulnerable children. Since then, we've grown into a global organization with a presence in 8 countries. Over the years, we've achieved many important milestones, such as sponsoring 1M children.

Financial Transparency

We believe that financial transparency is critical to building trust with our supporters. That's why we're committed to providing complete and accurate information about how we use donations. 88% of every dollar donated to Ray’s Charity goes directly to supporting our programs, and we maintain rigorous accounting and auditing practices to ensure accountability and transparency.

Our Team

Our charity is led by a dedicated team of professionals who bring years of experience and expertise to our work. Meet our staff members and volunteers who are committed to changing the lives of orphans and vulnerable children. From our CEO to our field workers, everyone at Ray’s is passionate about our mission and committed to making a difference.
