Our Work

Our Commitment to Children

Thank you for your interest in volunteering with our orphanage! We rely on volunteers like you to help us provide the best possible care for the children in our care. There are many ways you can get involved and make a difference in the lives of these wonderful children.

School enrollment

We enroll every child in our care in local schools, where they receive a formal education in a safe and supportive environment.

Homework assistance

We provide homework assistance to help our children succeed academically and build self-confidence.

Vocational training

For older children, we offer vocational training in a range of trades, such as carpentry, tailoring, and computer repair, to help them acquire the skills they need to succeed in the workforce.

Community Engagement

We believe that building strong relationships with our local community is essential to our success. Here are some of the ways we engage with the community:

Community events

We believe in the importance of building strong relationships with our local community. To that end, we organize community events such as fairs and festivals to promote awareness of our orphanage and the work we do, engaging with community members and businesses to secure support for our mission and the children in our care.

Partnerships

We partner with local organizations and businesses to raise awareness of our work and secure funding for our programs and services.

Volunteerism

We encourage community members to volunteer with us, helping to build relationships and support our mission.

Medical check-ups

We provide regular medical check-ups for every child in our care to ensure they are healthy and receiving the care they need.

Nutritious meals

We provide three nutritious meals a day to our children, ensuring they receive the proper nutrients to support their growth and development.

Health education

We provide health education to our children and their caregivers, helping them understand the importance of good hygiene, healthy eating, and physical activity.
