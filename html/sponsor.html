Sponsor A Child

How It Works

By sponsoring a child, you can provide the financial support needed to ensure that they receive food, shelter, medical care, and an education.
In return, you will receive regular updates on your sponsored child's progress and the knowledge that you are making a difference in their life.

Sponsership Details

The cost of sponsoring a child is $200 per month. This provides your sponsored child with basic necessities such as food, shelter, medical care, and education. You will receive regular updates on your sponsored child's progress, including photos and videos. Your sponsorship commitment lasts for 12 months and can be renewed at any time.

Questions Frequently Asked

How is my sponsorship money used?

Your sponsorship money is used to provide your sponsored child with food, shelter, medical care, and an education. Any additional funds are used to support the orphanage and its programs.

Can I visit my sponsored child?

Yes, we welcome visits from sponsors. However, please note that visits must be arranged in advance and are subject to certain conditions.

How often will I receive updates on my sponsored child?

You will receive regular updates on your sponsored child's progress, including photos and videos. These updates are provided on a weekly basis.
