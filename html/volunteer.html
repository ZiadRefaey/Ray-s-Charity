Volunteer

Volunteer with Us

Thank you for your interest in volunteering with our orphanage! We rely on volunteers like you to help us provide the best possible care for the children in our care. There are many ways you can get involved and make a difference in the lives of these wonderful children.

Volunteer Opportunities

Mentoring

Our children benefit greatly from positive adult role models. As a mentor, you can help our children build self-esteem and develop new skills by spending one-on-one time with them on a regular basis.

Tutoring

Many of our children struggle with academics due to a lack of resources or support. As a tutor, you can help our children improve their academic performance and achieve their full potential.

Fundraising

Our orphanage relies on donations to support the children in our care. As a fundraising volunteer, you can help us organize events, promote our cause, and raise funds to support our mission.

Administrative Support

We have a range of administrative tasks that need to be completed on a regular basis, such as data entry, filing, and answering phones. As an administrative volunteer, you can help us keep our operations running smoothly.

Requirements

Fill out our online application form

To become a volunteer, you'll need to fill out an online application form that collects basic information about you, your interests, and your availability. This helps us understand your background and goals so we can match you with the right volunteer opportunity.

Attend an orientation session

Once you've completed the application form, you'll need to attend an orientation session to learn more about our orphanage and our volunteer program. During the orientation, we'll provide you with an overview of our mission, policies, and procedures, as well as an introduction to the different volunteer opportunities available.

Pass a background check

For the safety and well-being of our children, we require all volunteers to pass a background check before they can begin volunteering. This helps us ensure that our volunteers have a clean criminal record and are not a risk to the children in our care.

Begin volunteering

Once you've completed the online application, attended the orientation session, and passed the background check, you're ready to begin volunteering! We'll work with you to identify the right volunteer opportunity based on your skills, interests, and availability, and provide you with the necessary training and support to help you succeed in your role.

If you have any questions about volunteering with us, please don't hesitate to get in touch! You can reach us by phone or email, or stop by our orphanage during our business hours. We look forward to hearing from you!
